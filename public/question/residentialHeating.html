How should homes be heated?

In 2007, 82% of homes were heated with gas boilers. It is expected that the 26 million heating systems in existing homes and the systems in 14 million new homes will need to be replaced by 2050. The 2050 calculator considers eleven technologies that could be used to replace existing heaters.
